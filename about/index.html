About me

Coach, student, and lifelong grappler.

I've trained Jiu Jitsu since 2014, around ten years, and I'm a purple belt. After a major knee surgery I had to rebuild my flexibility, mobility and strength from the ground up, so I know first-hand what it takes to come back as an athlete, and how to coach someone through it. More than anything, I love teaching, both classes and privates.

On the mat

Purple belt, training Jiu Jitsu since 2014 — about ten years of No-Gi and grappling.

Where I teach

Regular classes on Fridays at ASVZ BJJ and Thursdays at Stark Jiu Jitsu, plus privates around Zürich.

The comeback

I rebuilt my strength and mobility after major knee surgery, so I coach smart, injury-aware training.

Off the mat

I study medicine, which shapes how I think about movement, injury and recovery.

Around the world

I've lived in Brazil, Argentina and Nicaragua and trained in São Paulo. I speak Portuguese and Spanish.

Currently learning

Scuba diving and wood carving — so far I've carved a spoon and a butter knife.

I love, love, love teaching — classes and privates.
