Questions, answered

Everything worth knowing before your first class.

Do I need to be in shape before starting?
Not really. Getting in shape happens as you train.
Do you teach specific moves or the concepts behind them?
I teach principles. Once you understand the ‘why,’ you can apply the logic to several positions.
I’ve never done BJJ before. Can I still book a private?
Yes, that’s completely fine. We’ll start from the basics and go at your pace.
Will we do positional sparring or just static drilling?
We’ll use progressive resistance. You’ll test the moves against me to ensure they work under pressure.
What would I even work on in a private?
Totally up to you. If you’re unsure, we’ll start, spot what needs work, and build a plan around your goals.
Do you keep notes on our progress so we can pick up where we left off?
I keep a digital log for every private student to ensure we’re building a cohesive system.
Do I need to commit to multiple sessions?
You’ll get a trial class and then you’ll decide how many classes you want to do.
How flexible is scheduling?
Completely up to you. Anytime that suits your schedule.
What is your policy if I need to reschedule due to work or injury?
Just give me 24 hours’ notice and we’ll push it to next week. No charge.
Can I bring a specific video or move I’m struggling with to troubleshoot?
Absolutely. Send it over ahead of time and I’ll have a breakdown ready.
Where do you teach Nogi Jiu Jitsu in Zürich?
Sessions take place in a private setting in Zürich. Once you book, I’ll send you the exact location and directions.
Do I need a Gi to train?
No. We train Nogi, so a rash guard and shorts or leggings are all you need.
What should I bring to my first class?
Comfortable athletic clothing and water. No experience and no Gi needed, just a bit of curiosity.
Is Brazilian Jiu Jitsu suitable for complete beginners?
Yes. Every session is built around you, so beginners and experienced grapplers both train at their own pace.
Can I train semi-private with a friend or partner?
Yes. Semi-private is for two people who want to learn together and keep each other motivated.
Are there memberships or long contracts?
No memberships and no lock-in. You book the classes you want, when you want them.
